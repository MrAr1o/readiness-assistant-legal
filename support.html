Support

How to contact Readiness Assistant support.

Support

Readiness Assistant support is handled by SteikTech.

Contact

For product support, billing questions, installation help, or data requests, contact:

support@steiktech.de

For the initial test and beta phase, vulnerability reports are also handled through the same monitored inbox:

support@steiktech.de

Before public Marketplace launch, SteikTech may add a dedicated security inbox such as security@steiktech.de.

What To Include

When contacting support, include:

  • your Confluence site name;
  • the app environment if known;
  • the feature affected;
  • steps to reproduce the issue;
  • screenshots if helpful;
  • whether the issue affects a beta test, Marketplace review, or production use.

Do not send passwords, API keys, personal access tokens, or sensitive Confluence page content by email.

Expected Response

During beta, SteikTech aims to acknowledge support requests within 2 business days.

Security reports are prioritised and should receive an acknowledgement within 48 hours.
