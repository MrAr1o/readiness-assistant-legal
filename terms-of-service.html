2. Description of the App

Readiness Assistant is a Confluence Cloud tool that helps teams prepare documentation for audits, certifications, and enterprise reviews by:

  • scanning Confluence spaces to identify documentation gaps against compliance frameworks;
  • detecting stale, ownerless, and poorly structured documentation;
  • generating readiness scores, gap checklists, and remediation workflows;
  • producing internal Readiness Audit Packs for review purposes.

Important limitations

The App does not:

  • certify your organisation's compliance with any regulation, law, or standard;
  • constitute legal, regulatory, audit, or compliance advice;
  • replace qualified compliance professionals, auditors, or legal counsel;
  • guarantee audit success, regulatory approval, or certification;
  • provide any assurance that your organisation actually meets the requirements of any framework.

All readiness scores, gap analyses, and reports are generated by automated keyword-heuristic analysis. They reflect documentation *presence* — not documentation quality, accuracy, or sufficiency for any regulatory purpose. You remain solely responsible for your compliance obligations.
